Section 5.14

Slipstream

Slipstreaming is the act of integrating installations into a larger installation - most commonly, Updates into Install ISOs.

Integrating updates saves time, money, and gigabytes of internet transfer data by putting them right in the Windows 7 Installer so they're already installed by the time you see the desktop. The cost is in the size of the installer, more specifically the Windows Image. There is also some increase in installation time, and it may be necessary to Split the installer into more manageable pieces.

The Slipstream Process can take many hours to complete, and may require significant temporary storage space, but Slips7ream tries to make the process easy and convenient, with settings and automations to simplify your experience.
