Section 1.4

Service Pack

The Service Pack field(s) let you integrate Service Pack 1 into Windows 7. If you have both 32-Bit and 64-Bit Image Packages in the Image Packages List, you will notice two fields instead of just one: one for the x86 Service Pack EXE, the other for the x64 EXE.

Note: x64 Integration requires a 64-Bit Operating System.
