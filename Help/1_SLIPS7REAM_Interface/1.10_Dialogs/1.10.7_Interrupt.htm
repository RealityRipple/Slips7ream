Section 1.10.7

Cancel

Slips7ream is busy slipstreaming updates and packages

Clicking the "Cancel" button during the Integration process will prompt you to confirm your wish to stop. If you hit "Yes", all active processes will be stopped. If you are saving your image to an ISO or simply using an ISO source, all progress will be lost and you will have to start the integration process again. When working with a WIM directly, any changes that have already been made and saved will still be applied, but any active changes will not. This means that if you cancel during Update integration, any Service Pack integration and Image Package merging or removing will have already been applied.

If you click on Slips7ream's Close button (), you will also be prompted to confirm the action as above. This is to prevent accidental closures for your convenience.

Slips7ream is busy extracting and reading Image Package data

Clicking the "Cancel" button during the Loading Package Data process will prompt you to confirm your wish to stop. If you hit "Yes", Slips7ream will not populate the Image Packages list, and you will not be able to begin the Slipstream process until the Package Data is reloaded.

If you click on Slips7ream's Close button (), you will also be prompted to confirm the action as above. This is to prevent accidental closures for your convenience.

Slips7ream is busy extracting and reading Windows Feature data

Clicking the "Cancel" button during the Loading Windows Features process will prompt you to confirm your wish to stop. If you hit "Yes", Slips7ream will stop parsing Windows Features and you will have to Re-Parse the Windows Features that were interrupted if you wish to work with them.

If you click on Slips7ream's Close button (), you will also be prompted to confirm the action as above. This is to prevent accidental closures for your convenience.

Slips7ream is busy extracting and reading Integrated Windows Update data

Clicking the "Cancel" button during the Loading Integrated Windows Updates process will prompt you to confirm your wish to stop. If you hit "Yes", Slips7ream will stop parsing Integrated Updates and you will have to Re-Parse the Integrated Windows Updates that were interrupted if you wish to work with them.

If you click on Slips7ream's Close button (), you will also be prompted to confirm the action as above. This is to prevent accidental closures for your convenience.

Slips7ream is busy extracting and reading Integrated Driver data

Clicking the "Cancel" button during the Loading Integrated Drivers process will prompt you to confirm your wish to stop. If you hit "Yes", Slips7ream will stop parsing Integrated Drivers and you will have to Re-Parse the Integrated Drivers that were interrupted if you wish to work with them.

If you click on Slips7ream's Close button (), you will also be prompted to confirm the action as above. This is to prevent accidental closures for your convenience.

Slips7ream is busy extracting and reading Update data

Clicking the "Cancel" button during the Loading Updates process will prompt you to confirm your wish to stop. If you hit "Yes", Slips7ream will stop parsing Updates and you will have to Re-Add any Updates you wish to include. Any updates included before you hit Cancel will still be included in the Updates list, but can be removed through the interface.

If you click on Slips7ream's Close button (), you will also be prompted to confirm the action as above. This is to prevent accidental closures for your convenience.
