Section 1.10.3

Error when Opening Folder

If you receive the Error message "Unable to find completed Image" after clicking the "Open Folder" button, chances are the Finalized ISO or the Primary WIM is not where it was. It may have been moved or deleted, or SLIPS7REAM may not have access to the drive or path. It may also indicate that there was an undetected problem with the Slipstream Process.

If you receive the Error message "Unable to open folder" after clicking the "Open Folder" button, you can see the exact error by clicking on the Details button in the dialog. Generally, this error means that Windows Explorer failed to open the folder or SLIPS7REAM failed to open Windows Explorer.
